What does this mean (You have been assigned a task )?

Being assigned a task mean that you have been tasked as the responsible person to oversee the completion of the designated task. To complete the task you must ensure that the following is complete

1. Undertake the activity assigned in the task in the allotted time period.

2. Attach any documentation, printout or reports as necessary to the change control as evidence of the task completion.

3. Provide a summary of the task if required either attached to the change control or in the comment section. In this section you should prior releveant information, observations or conclusions.

4. Enter additional comments to provide clarity of the task or findings.

5. On completion of the task

a. Change the status to task complete

b. Notify the person responsible for the change control that the task is completed
